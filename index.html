Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

NotiFly ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

SMS and Notifications: To provide our core services (summarization and organization), NotiFly requires access to your SMS messages and system notifications. This data is used solely to generate summaries and help you manage your alerts.

2. How We Process Your Data (Hybrid AI)

We use a dual-layer AI system to protect your privacy:

  • Eight 1.0 (Offline Mode): Your data is processed entirely on your device. No information is sent to any servers.
  • Eight 2.0 (Online Mode): If you choose the Online AI, relevant message snippets are sent to our secure workers to generate summaries. We do not store this data permanently, and it is not used to train global AI models.

3. Permissions

NotiFly requests the following permissions:

  • RECEIVE_SMS & READ_SMS: To read and summarize incoming text messages.
  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS: To show you summarized alerts.
  • INTERNET: To provide Online AI features and AdMob support.

4. Third-Party Services

The app uses third-party services that may collect information used to identify you:

  • Google Play Services
  • AdMob

5. Data Retention

We do not collect or store your personal message data on our servers. Any message history is stored locally on your device and is deleted if you uninstall the app or clear its data.
