By using the Service, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Local Data Processing (No Remote Collection)

Privacy is a core focus of NotiFly. We do not transmit, collect, or store your personal data on any external servers. All core functionality is designed to operate locally on your device.

To provide our Service, the app requires access to the following, which are processed strictly on your device:

Names: You may enter a name to personalize your experience. This is saved only in your device's local storage.

Notifications: NotiFly requires access to your device's notifications to function. This data is read and processed locally and never leaves your phone.

Photos/Camera: We may request access to your device's photo library or camera for specific app features. Any photos accessed or taken remain on your device.

Because this data never leaves your device, we do not share, sell, or disclose your personal information to anyone.

2. Third-Party Services (Advertising)

While the core functionality of NotiFly processes data locally, the app does utilize a third-party service to display advertisements, which requires an active internet connection.

Google AdMob:
We use Google AdMob to serve ads. While we do not share your personal data (like names or photos) with AdMob, the AdMob SDK automatically collects certain device information (such as your IP address and mobile advertising identifiers) to provide and manage ads.

You can learn more about how Google handles data by reading the Google Privacy & Terms.

3. Security

Your data is secured by your device's native operating system protections. Because your personal data (names, notifications, photos) is not transmitted over the internet by our Service, it is protected from external breaches on our end. We encourage users to utilize standard device security measures, such as screen locks and encryption, to protect the data stored locally on their devices.

4. Children's Privacy

NotiFly is intended for a general audience and is safe for users of all ages, including children under 13, because we do not remotely collect or store any personal data. All user interactions, names, and photos remain on the user's device.

However, because we use Google AdMob, ads will be served to the device. We comply with standard developer policies regarding advertising to ensure that any ads served are appropriate, and we encourage parents to manage their children's device-level advertising preferences.
